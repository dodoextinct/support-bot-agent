# Drawing Tools

## Are indicators and drawings on charts free or paid?

All the indicators are absolutely free on the Angel One.

---

## Why are my drawings not getting retained on charts?

All Drawings under templates are stored inside the local storage. If you login through other devices you won’t be able to view previously saved drawings on other device/browser.

---

## Where to find Drawing tools on charts?

On Web or on Mobile platforms, click on blue arrow button that is present on left side of the charts – by clicking on it, it will give an expanded view of the drawing tools available
